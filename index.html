How We came to BEE:

On the eve of a particularly trying day our founder, Bill Durr, went out for a long walk and stopped to smell the roses. Unfortunately the rose he decided to smell contained a tiny little menace who flew into his nose and stung him. From that day on, he swore not only to take revenge on that vile species, but to bring the sweet fruit of his labour to the masses. And so, Bill's Bees was born. Our company seeks to bring you the highest quality honey, made from the succulent ichor of mashed bees.

The Product

At Bill's Bees, we use an innovative three step process to procure our top of the line honey

Step 1

Locate Bees

Our bees are one hundred percent wild, making our honey the most natural in the world! While we could be farming them, this would be in conflict with our mission to erase all bees.

Step 2

Burn The Hive

Not only does this allow up to eliminate large amounts of bees, it makes the surrounding area uninhabitable for any future bee colonies. We burn just enough to kill them, but not so much as to incinerate them.

Step 3

Mash The Bees

Our specialized bee mashers are some of the best in the business! Using their university bee mashing degrees, they are able to mash even mildly chard bees into fresh, goopy, most delicious honey!

The Vision

Our vision is the same as that of our founding fathers. A land of peace. A world where people can just stop and fearlessly smell the roses. A world without wars. A world without wars or drugs. A world with no disease or wars or drugs. A world sans violence and without drugs or wars or even disease. A world where a man can raise his child without pain or wars, or even disease and drugs. A land of hope and opportunity, without drugs or pain or disease or wars, and yes, even violence.

We, above all wish for a bee-less world. Because the only things sweet about bees are their mashed corpses.
